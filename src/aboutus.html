ABOUT COMPANY

SP Industries is a manufacturer of plastic packaging products based in Pune. Established in 2017, it is one of the leading manufacturing companies in the region. The company operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and is a key supplier to Supreme Industries. With investments in advanced technology and equipment, SP Industries ensures high-quality production for various industries. The company prioritizes sustainability, minimizing environmental impact while offering value-added services such as printing, labeling, and product design. Quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction remain at the forefront, supported by an efficient district-wide distribution network.
